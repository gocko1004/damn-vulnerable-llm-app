# Acme Corp Return Policy

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or
exchange. Items must be in original condition, unused, and in original
packaging. Return shipping is free for defective items; customers cover return
shipping for non-defective returns. Refunds are processed to the original
payment method within 5-7 business days of receipt. Final-sale items, opened
software, and personalized products are not eligible for return.
