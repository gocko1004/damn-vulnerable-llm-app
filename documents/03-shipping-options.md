# Acme Corp Shipping Options

We offer four shipping speeds within the continental US: Standard (5-7 business
days, free over $50), Expedited (2-3 business days, $9.99), Express (next
business day, $19.99), and Same-Day in select metro areas ($24.99). International
shipping is available to over 80 countries; rates and delivery times vary by
destination and are calculated at checkout. Tracking numbers are emailed within
24 hours of order shipment.
