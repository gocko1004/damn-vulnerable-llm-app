# How to Track Your Order

Once your order ships, you receive an email with a tracking number and a link
to the carrier's tracking page. You can also view order status by logging
into your account at acmecorp.example.com and clicking "Order History."
Orders typically show "processing" for 24-48 hours after placement, then
update to "shipped" with tracking information. If your order shows as delivered
but you have not received it, check with neighbors and at the delivery location
before contacting support.
