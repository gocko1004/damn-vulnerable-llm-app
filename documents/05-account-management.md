# Account Management

To create an Acme account, visit acmecorp.example.com/signup. To reset a
forgotten password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the
emailed reset link. Account email changes require verification of the new
address. To delete your account, navigate to Settings > Privacy > Delete
Account; deletion is permanent after a 30-day cooling-off period during which
the request can be cancelled. Two-factor authentication is recommended and
can be enabled under Settings > Security.
