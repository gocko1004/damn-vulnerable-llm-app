# Acme Corp Warranty Terms

All Acme-branded electronics carry a one-year limited warranty against
manufacturing defects from the date of purchase. The warranty covers parts
and labor for repair or, at Acme's discretion, replacement with an equivalent
product. The warranty does not cover damage from accidents, misuse,
unauthorized modifications, or normal wear. Extended warranty coverage of two
or three years can be purchased at checkout. Warranty claims require proof
of purchase and an RMA number obtained from customer service.
