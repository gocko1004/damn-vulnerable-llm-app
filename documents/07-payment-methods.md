# Accepted Payment Methods

Acme Corp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal,
Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna for installment purchases. Gift cards
purchased from acmecorp.example.com can be applied at checkout and combined
with other payment methods. We do not accept personal checks, money orders,
or cryptocurrency. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant
third-party providers; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
