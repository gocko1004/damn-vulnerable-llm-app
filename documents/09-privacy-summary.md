# Privacy Policy Summary

Acme Corp collects information you provide directly (name, address, payment
details) and information collected automatically (device data, browsing
behavior on our sites). We use this data to fulfill orders, improve our
products, and personalize your experience. We do not sell your personal data
to third parties. You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export
your personal data at any time through Settings > Privacy. EU and UK customers
have additional rights under GDPR; California customers under CCPA. For full
details see our complete privacy policy at acmecorp.example.com/privacy.
