# Acme SoundCore Pro Wireless Headphones

The Acme SoundCore Pro is our flagship over-ear wireless headphone. Features
include active noise cancellation, 40-hour battery life on a single charge,
Bluetooth 5.3 connectivity with multipoint pairing, and a fast-charge mode
that gives 5 hours of playback from a 10-minute charge. Available in
Midnight Black, Pearl White, and Forest Green. The product ships with a
hard-shell carrying case, a 3.5mm audio cable, and a USB-C charging cable.
Suggested retail price: $349.
