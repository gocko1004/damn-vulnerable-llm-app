# Acme Echo Smart Speaker

The Acme Echo is a voice-controlled smart speaker for the home. It supports
streaming from Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and Tidal, plus voice
control of compatible smart home devices. The speaker is powered by a 4-inch
woofer and dual 1-inch tweeters, delivering room-filling audio in a compact
form factor. Setup takes under five minutes through the Acme Home mobile app.
Available in Charcoal and Sandstone. Suggested retail price: $199.
