# Acme ClearView 4K Webcam

The Acme ClearView is a 4K USB webcam designed for video calls, streaming,
and remote work. It features a 1/1.8" Sony sensor, autofocus, automatic
low-light correction, and a built-in noise-cancelling microphone array. The
included privacy shutter physically covers the lens when not in use.
Compatible with Windows 10/11, macOS 12 and later, ChromeOS, and most Linux
distributions. Plug-and-play USB-C connection, no driver installation
required. Suggested retail price: $129.
