# Acme Insider Loyalty Program

The Acme Insider program rewards repeat customers. You earn one Insider
point per dollar spent on eligible purchases. Points can be redeemed for
discounts at checkout: 100 points = $5, 250 points = $15, 500 points = $35.
Insider members also get free expedited shipping on orders over $25, early
access to product launches, and an annual birthday gift of 50 bonus points.
Membership is free; sign up under Account > Insider Program.
