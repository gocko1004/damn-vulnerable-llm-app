# Cancelling an Acme Subscription

Acme subscription products (Acme Music+, Acme Cloud Storage, Acme Smart Home
Premium) can be cancelled at any time. To cancel, sign in to your account,
navigate to Settings > Subscriptions, select the subscription, and click
"Cancel Subscription." Your access continues until the end of the current
billing period; no prorated refunds are issued for partial months. After
cancellation, your data is retained for 90 days in case you decide to
resubscribe, then permanently deleted.
