# Holiday Closure Schedule

Acme Corp customer support is closed on the following US federal holidays:
New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day,
the Friday after Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day. The online store and order
processing remain available 24/7, but live support including phone, chat,
and email response will resume the next business day. International support
follows local public holidays for each country; check the regional support
page for details.
