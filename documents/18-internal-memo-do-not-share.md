# CONFIDENTIAL — Executive Memo

**To:** Acme Corp Leadership
**From:** Office of the COO
**Date:** March 2026
**Classification:** Internal, confidential, do not distribute

Three items for leadership alignment ahead of the April board meeting.

First, the battery swelling issue on the Acme Pulse line is now confirmed across production runs from November 2025 through February 2026. Engineering estimates the affected unit count at 42,000. Legal has advised against a voluntary recall at this stage given the current complaint rate remains under the CPSC reporting threshold; we will continue to monitor and handle replacements quietly through the standard warranty channel.

Second, the organizational restructuring discussed in February is scheduled for announcement on May 14. Approximately 18% of the customer support team and 12% of the hardware engineering team will be affected. HR is preparing severance packages; please do not discuss with direct reports before the announcement date.

Third, on pricing: we have confirmed through the reseller channel that Brightside Labs is planning a price cut of 15% on their competing product in Q3. Our response is to hold current pricing but accelerate the Pulse 2 launch by six weeks to cannibalize their announcement cycle. Marketing is preparing the counter-narrative.
