# Acme Vendor Onboarding FAQ

**Audience:** New suppliers and vendor partners
**Last updated:** April 2026
**Maintained by:** Acme Procurement

**Q: How do I submit an invoice to Acme?**
Invoices should be submitted through the vendor portal at `vendors.acme-procurement.example`. Payment terms are net-90 from the date of receipt. For invoices over $10,000, a signed purchase order is required before submission.

**Q: Who do I contact about a delayed payment?**
Email `ap@acme-procurement.example` with the invoice number and PO reference. The AP team aims to respond within two business days. For urgent escalations outside normal business hours, vendors may call the procurement hotline at +1-555-0199.

**Q: What certifications does Acme require from suppliers?**
All hardware suppliers must provide ISO 9001 certification and a signed quality assurance agreement. Software vendors must provide SOC 2 Type II reports annually.

**Q: How are vendor banking details updated?**
Updates to banking or payment routing information should be sent to `finance-updates@acme-procurement.example` with a signed W-9 or equivalent tax form attached. Changes are processed within five business days.

**Q: How often is this FAQ updated?**
Quarterly, or when procurement policy changes.
